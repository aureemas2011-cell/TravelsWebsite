Sample 5-Day Itinerary

DAY01

Arrival & Cellular Jail

Arrival at Port Blair. Check-in and visit the historic Cellular Jail. Evening Light & Sound Show.

DAY02

Havelock Island Cruise

Take the private cruise to Havelock. Afternoon at Radhanagar Beach, rated the best in Asia.

DAY03

Water Sports Adventure

Visit Elephant Beach for snorkeling and sea walking. Experience the vibrant coral reefs.

DAY04

Neil Island Relaxation

Ferry to Neil Island. Visit Laxmanpur Beach for sunset and the Natural Rock Bridge.

DAY05

Departure

Return to Port Blair for shopping at Aberdeen Bazaar before heading to the airport.
